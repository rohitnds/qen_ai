Programs Designed Around
AI Researchers

Research Ventures Program

We collaborate with university researchers to commercialize high-potential AI innovations through joint SBIR/STTR proposals and industry-aligned pilots. From prototype development and market discovery to company formation, Qen Labs is a long-term partner in the full commercialization journey.

Learn More

Startup Postdoctoral Program

Designed for recent Ph.D. graduates in AI or computational sciences, this program offers a structured, low-risk path into entrepreneurship. Participants join Qen Labs as Principal Investigators, leveraging their own research while pursuing SBIR/STTR Phase I and II awards.

Learn More

Predoctoral Program

Our predoctoral fellows work on mission-aligned, real-world AI challenges that evolve into their PhD research topics with our academic partners. Employed by Qen Labs, they gain industry experience, contribute to live projects in defense, healthcare, urban computing, or energy.

Learn More

We build ventures at the intersection of AI, complex systems, and societal resilience.
